Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for custom designs. However, we do accommodate rush requests on a case-by-case basis depending on availability.

Do you offer color customization?

Absolutely! All our packages are fully customizable. Share your color preferences, theme, or inspiration, and we'll create something perfect for your event.

Is setup and teardown included?

Yes! Professional delivery, setup, and teardown are included with all our packages. We handle everything so you can enjoy your event stress-free.

What areas do you service?

We service the Marietta, Kennesaw, Atlanta, and Woodstock areas. Contact us to confirm coverage for your location and discuss any travel fees if applicable.

Can you work with my budget?

We love working with various budgets! Mix and match our packages and add-ons, or contact us to discuss custom solutions that fit your needs.

What deposit is required to book?

50% deposit required to reserve your date. The balance is due 1 week before your event. We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience.
