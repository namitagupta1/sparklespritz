Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance for custom designs. However, we do accommodate rush requests on a case-by-case basis depending on availability.

Do you offer color customization?

Absolutely! All our packages are fully customizable. Share your color preferences, theme, or inspiration, and we'll create something perfect for your event.

Is setup and teardown included?

Yes! Professional delivery, setup, and teardown are included with all our packages. We handle everything so you can enjoy your event stress-free.
